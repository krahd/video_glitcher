Quick Start

The workflow is intentionally short: load, tune, preview, export. `ffmpeg` must be available on your system `PATH` for MP4 export.

  1. Launch the app and load a video with the button, a click on the empty state, or the L key.
  2. Pick a preset such as Cinematic, Broken Codec, or Extreme.
  3. Tune intensity, frequency, burst timing, and individual effect toggles while the preview is running.
  4. Use G to toggle glitching, Space to pause, and F to freeze on a frame.
  5. Press E or click Export Start to render the current look to MP4.

Core Controls

L load video Space play / pause G glitch on / off F freeze frame H HUD U GUI E export S save PNG frame
